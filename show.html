Yugam Event Information

Yugam is a highly anticipated event organized by Kumaraguru Institute of Technology in Coimbatore. It is a technical and cultural festival that will take place from 22nd September 2023 to 24th September 2023. The event has received Approval from the Institute and requires IRA (Internal Review and Assessment Yugam offers a wide range of activities, including technical competitions, workshops, guest lectures, and cultural performances. It aims to promote innovation, creativity, and talent among participating students. The event promises an exciting and fulfilling experience for attendees, providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and healthy competition.
